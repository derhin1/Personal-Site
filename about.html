In my freetime, these are some of my favorite activities.

Board Games / Video Games

I have been playing video games since I was a kid and they have been a big part of my life. One of my favorites is League of Legends because of its replayability. League of Legends is an ever changing game where no two games are the same. The enemy team and your team are both random variables and that impacts how the game plays out. That being said, it doesn’t mean that who wins is a coin flip, I love the game because it is up to you and your team to work together to overcome the challenges that may arise.

In a similar vein, I also love playing board games with my friends. An ideal night for me would be a gathering with friends and just playing board games. There are many genres for board games and I honestly love playing and learning any kind. For example, I was recently given a board game called Gloomhaven. It is a massive game with the box weighing almost twenty pounds! The rules were also just as complicated as you’d imagine a twenty pound box would be but I loved every second of trying to learn the game with my friends. As I mentioned above, I do very much enjoy cooperative games where you work together with your friends, however the competitive side of me also enjoys playing board games where you compete with each other.

Puzzles

Puzzles are something that I enjoy struggling through. It is always satisfying finally figuring something out after being stumped for a long time. One example that I have recently been playing is a daily word game called Wordle that can be found here. The goal of the game is to figure out what the five-letter word of the day is, however the catch is that you only get six attempts. Whenever you input a word it will tell you whether certain letters are used in the word of the day and if they are, if the position of the letter is right or not. I have been loving the game because my friends and I have been competing to see who can figure out the word in the least number of tries.

Another example of puzzles that I love are escape rooms. Escape rooms are locations that you go to where they put you and your friends in a “locked” room and the only way to escape is to solve a series of puzzles before a set time limit which is typically an hour. I enjoy these rooms because when you first enter a room it can be almost overwhelming how many puzzles there are in a room but after solving some, everything else starts to make sense. It is a lot of fun to do these with friends and I highly recommend it to anyone that hasn’t tried it yet!

Climbing

Occasionally I like to go climbing! I personally find it a lot more engaging and fun than doing a regular workout. Climbing gyms have a large variety of different walls and each of them can have multiple ways to scale the wall up such as only being able to use green rocks. Climbing is neat because there is a lot more technique to it than one might think. I am definitely not a strong person however that doesn’t stop me from being able to climb. I use my hands to keep myself balanced while I put my foot on a higher hold and then push up with my legs to climb. This won’t work on every wall or challenge as some do and will require you to pull yourself up however this is where having a belayer is nice! Not only do they keep you safe by preventing you from falling but they also allow you to rest mid-climb as they can hold you in place.
